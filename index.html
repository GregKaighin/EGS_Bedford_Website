About Us

EGS Bedford is a family-run electrical and gas services company based in Bedfordshire, serving homeowners, landlords and businesses across the region. We offer a comprehensive range of services — from full electrical rewires and consumer unit upgrades to boiler installations, central heating systems and landlord safety certificates. Whether you need a straightforward repair or a complete installation from scratch, our team brings the same level of care and professionalism to every job.

All of our engineers are fully qualified and registered with the relevant industry bodies — NICEIC for electrical work and Gas Safe for all gas and heating installations. This means you can book with confidence, knowing that every job meets current safety standards and is fully compliant with UK regulations.


We work across a wide range of projects, from single-room upgrades in domestic properties to large-scale commercial and industrial installations. No job is too big or too small, and we always aim to complete work on time, on budget and with minimal disruption to you.

Stuart Millington

Founder & Lead Engineer

Stuart founded EGS Bedford with a commitment to delivering reliable, high-quality electrical and gas services across Bedfordshire. With over 20 years of hands-on experience in domestic, commercial and industrial installations, Stuart leads every project with a focus on safety, precision and customer satisfaction.

Fully qualified and registered with both NICEIC and Gas Safe, Stuart and his team take pride in their workmanship and the lasting relationships they build with clients throughout the region.
